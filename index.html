About Me

Benjamin Adedowole

Cybersecurity & Software Developer

I am a Computer Science and Cyber Operations student at Dakota State University. Beyond academics, I actively participate in cybersecurity competitions such as the US Cyber Games, MetaCTF, HackTheBox University CTF, and TryHackMe’s Advent of Cyber.

I’ve built projects ranging from PyPikachu, an AI-powered Pokémon card defect detection pipeline, to Clash of Prodigies, a STEM competition platform for high school students in Nigeria. I also maintain a collection of Discord bots and have developed a terminal-based game suite in C using ncurses.

My passion lies in combining secure development practices with creative problem solving to deliver solutions that scale.
